TEAM SEAS

Team Seas, is an international collaborative fundraiser project run by YouTubers MrBeast and Mark Rober as a follow up to Team Trees. They have succeeded in raising 30 million U.S. dollars, pledging to remove 30,000,000 pounds of marine debris from the ocean by the end of 2021
